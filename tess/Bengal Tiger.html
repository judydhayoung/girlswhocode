The Bengal Tiger

The Bengal Tiger is a mammal that is that is part of the big cats. These animals live in India. They are predators and are on the top of the Indian forests food chain.
These animals are carnivores meaning that they only eat meat. These animals eat animals such as water buffalo, deer, wild boar, and more.


Diet


These carnivores eat meat in the Indian rain-forests such as Water buffalo, Indian deer, Indian wild boar, and most of the animals of the forest.
